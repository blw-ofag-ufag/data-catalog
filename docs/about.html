Über diesen Datenkatalog

Was genau ist ein Datenkatalog? Und wie soll er uns bei der digitalen Transformation unterstützen? Antworten auf diese Fragen findest du hier.

What Is a Data Catalog?

A data catalog is a centralized repository that describes the data your organization manages. Think of it like a library card catalog, but for digital information rather than books. It helps you see at a glance what data exists, where it is stored, who owns it, and how it can be accessed.

Why Does It Matter?

With a well-maintained catalog, the Federal Office for Agriculture gains a single source of truth for all data assets. This increases efficiency, boosts collaboration between teams, and ensures everyone follows the same governance and compliance rules. When data is easy to find and understand, it becomes simpler to share it responsibly and foster informed decision-making.

Our Goals

The data catalog is designed to provide:

  • Holistic Data View: A clear, user-friendly overview of all datasets, ensuring that important information is always at your fingertips.
  • Ownership Clarity: Each dataset is linked to its respective data owner, making it easy to direct questions and requests.
  • Accessible and Secure Sharing: We aim to align with national platforms (like i14y) and open data portals, so valuable data can be shared more widely and securely.
  • Foundations for Advanced Analytics: Over time, we plan to adopt AI-driven features that uncover patterns, trends, and insights from these datasets.

Key Features

The catalog highlights essential information about each dataset, such as its title, description, update frequency, and any relevant access restrictions. In addition, it supports:

  1. Consistent Metadata: Easily reference attributes like publishing date, languages, and owner details.
  2. Compliance Ready: Each entry labels data as public, restricted, or confidential, simplifying adherence to privacy and security guidelines.
  3. Future Integrations: We aim to enable seamless data flows (via APIs) and reduce manual work for updates or new publications.

Who Benefits?

Everyone at the Federal Office for Agriculture can gain something from the data catalog:

  • Management and Policy Makers: Quickly find the data they need for evidence-based strategies.
  • Data Owners and Analysts: Know exactly who is responsible for various datasets and avoid duplication of effort.
  • Technical Teams: More efficiently plan system integrations and maintain data quality.
  • External Stakeholders: Where relevant, access published datasets and leverage them for research or joint projects.

Next Steps

We will continue refining the catalog by reaching out to data owners, updating existing entries, and exploring integrations with other platforms. Over time, we aim to make this tool an indispensable resource for both internal and external partners, enabling data-driven innovation across the board.
